A New Standard in Maternal Health

Amrita proposes a comprehensive system to address preeclampsia, a critical condition affecting pregnant individuals. Our approach integrates cutting-edge technology with user-centric design to provide timely insights and support.

Home Monitoring

Safe and accurate data collection from the comfort of home.

AI-Powered Analysis

Intelligent algorithms for early risk stratification and alerts.

Integrated Care

Seamless connection between patients and healthcare providers.

For Patients: User-Friendly Monitoring

Our app is designed to make daily monitoring simple and accessible for pregnant individuals.

BP Entry

Manual input or camera-based scan from BP monitors for accurate readings.

Proteinuria Test

Enter dipstick results via smartphone camera interpretation or color code selection.

Symptom Questionnaire

AI-guided questions for comprehensive symptom assessment (headache, swelling, etc.).

Biomarker Input

Simple entry for PLGF/sFLt results from LFIA kits, crucial for risk assessment.

Health Education

Short animated reels on preeclampsia, kit usage, and when to seek hospital care.

Smart Reminders

Daily/weekly reminders for BP checks, protein tests, medication, and ANC appointments.

For Healthcare Workers: The Clinical Hub

A powerful interface designed to support doctors, ANMs, and ASHA workers in managing patient care.

Patient Dashboard

A clear overview highlighting high-risk patients and their current status.

Trends & History

Detailed graphs for BP and biomarker trends, along with complete patient history.

Teleconsultation

Integrated system for remote consultations and advice sharing.

Advice & Management

Doctors can easily upload advice, prescriptions, and manage follow-ups.
